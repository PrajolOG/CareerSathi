Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how Career Sathi collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our AI career counseling platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, build your profile, or interact with our AI assistant:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Profile Data: Academic level, grades, streams, entrance scores, and personal interests.
  • Chat Data: Text conversations between you and the Career Sathi AI assistant.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you navigate and interact with the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to provide a personalized experience and improve our services:

  • To generate customized career roadmaps and recommendations.
  • To provide conversational AI support tailored to your academic background.
  • To analyze platform usage trends and technical performance.
  • To communicate with you about your account or service updates.

3. AI Data Processing & Analysis

Career Sathi uses advanced AI models to analyze your data. Please note:

  • Your profile and chat data are passed to AI models (e.g., via secure APIs) to generate specialized advice.
  • We use anonymized data to fine-tune our internal recommendation engines and improve the quality of roadmaps.
  • We do NOT sell your personal chat history or academic data to educational marketers or third-party advertisers.

4. Data Storage & Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • Data is stored in secure, encrypted cloud databases (hosted by providers like Supabase).
  • We use industry-standard authentication (OAuth/JWT) to ensure only you can access your private data.
  • While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Rights & Control

You have full control over your data on Career Sathi:

  • Access: You can view all your profile and chat data directly in the application.
  • Delete: You can clear your chat history or delete your entire account at any time from the Settings menu.
  • Export: You can request a copy of your stored data for your own records.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We only share data with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share data with infrastructure partners (like cloud hosting and AI API providers) necessary to run the service.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, we may disclose information to public authorities.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to maintain your login session and enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Used for authentication and security.
  • Functional Cookies: Used to remember your site preferences (like tab selections).

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out to us:

  • Email: privacy@careersathi.com
